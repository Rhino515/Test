About the Creator ⚡

Hi, my name is Andrew and I work as a professional trimmer at NorCal Cannabis. I created the NorCal Trim Calculator to help simplify bonus calculations and make it easier for trimmers to track their daily progress.

This tool is designed to be practical, easy to use, and efficient — helping workers stay organized and informed. My goal is to support the trimming community with simple tech tools that really make a difference on the job.
